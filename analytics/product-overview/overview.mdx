Product Overview gives you a complete summary of how your app or website is performing.

Use this page to quickly understand user activity, traffic sources, engagement quality, retention, app versions, permissions, and growth from one place.

## How to use Product Overview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select date range">
    Choose Today, Yesterday, 7 Days, or a custom date range based on the period you want to review.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose country">
    Select All countries or a specific country to compare product performance by region.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start with the top metrics">
    Review new users, active users, retention users, and average session duration to understand product health.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check traffic and engagement">
    Review acquisition source, open frequency, average page views, and notification permissions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review retention and growth">
    Check user retention, version distribution, and app growth to understand long-term product quality.
  </Step>
</Steps>
