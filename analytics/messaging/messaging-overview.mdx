# Messaging Overview

Messaging helps you communicate with your app users through targeted push notification campaigns.

Use Messaging when you want to send offers, reminders, updates, alerts, product announcements, or re-engagement messages to selected users.

<Note>
  Messaging is designed to help teams send the right message to the right users at the right time.
</Note>

---

## What is Messaging?

Messaging is the communication center of Twinalyze.

It connects your analytics data with push notification campaigns, so you can target users based on behavior, events, language, country, device properties, or other tracked information.

## What you can send

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Promotional offers">
    Send discounts, flash sales, seasonal campaigns, and limited-time deals to selected users.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Product updates">
    Announce new features, app changes, improvements, or important releases.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Re-engagement messages">
    Bring inactive users back by targeting users who have not returned or completed an important action.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reminders">
    Send reminders for incomplete actions, pending tasks, subscriptions, orders, bookings, or app activity.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Localized campaigns">
    Send language-wise messages so users receive content that feels more relevant and understandable.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## When to use Messaging

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Marketing">
    Use Messaging to send offers, announcements, festive campaigns, new arrivals, and promotional updates.

    <Tip>
      Best for increasing campaign reach, user reactivation, and offer visibility.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Product">
    Use Messaging to inform users about new features, app updates, important changes, or product education.

    <Tip>
      Best for improving feature adoption and helping users discover important app areas.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Engagement">
    Use Messaging to bring users back after inactivity or guide them toward the next useful action.

    <Tip>
      Best for improving repeat usage and reducing drop-off.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Operations">
    Use Messaging to send important reminders, alerts, order updates, service updates, or time-sensitive information.

    <Tip>
      Best for keeping users informed at the right moment.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
