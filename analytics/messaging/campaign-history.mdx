## Common use cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check whether a campaign was sent">
    Use the Status column to confirm whether the campaign is Sent, Scheduled, Failed, Draft, or Cancelled.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Find failed campaigns">
    Use the Status filter or Failed column to find campaigns that need delivery review.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Compare campaign engagement">
    Use Opened and Click Rate to compare which campaigns performed better.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reuse a campaign">
    Use Duplicate when you want to create a similar campaign without starting from zero.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Create a new campaign">
    Click New Campaign to start a new push notification campaign.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
