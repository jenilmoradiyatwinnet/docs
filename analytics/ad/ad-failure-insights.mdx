Ad Failure Insights helps you understand **where ads are failing**, **which ad formats are affected**, and **which screen or error code needs attention**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review ad company failure cards">
    The top section shows failure cards for each ad company, such as **Admob**, **Facebook**, and **Adx**.

    Each card gives a quick summary of failed ad activity. You can compare total users, total failed events, average failure value, and format-wise failure distribution.

## How to use Ad Failure Insights

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select filters">
    Choose date range, app name, testing device, and app version.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Compare ad companies">
    Start with the top cards to see which ad company has the highest failed events or average value.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the failing format">
    Review format-level data to identify whether Native, App Open, Banner, Interstitial, MREC, or In-feed is failing most.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Total Errors">
    Expand the ad format and error code to view exact failure messages.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Screen Info">
    Review which screens are connected to the ad failures.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share details with developers">
    Copy the error message, error code, app version, screen name, and affected format for debugging.
  </Step>
</Steps>
