# Ad Report

Ad Report helps you review ad performance across countries, ad networks, and ad formats.

Use this page to understand how many ads were requested, loaded, shown, failed, and clicked. You can also export the report as **PDF** or **CSV** for sharing and analysis.

---

## Page preview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Filter ad report data">
    The top filter section helps you choose the exact ad data you want to review.

    You can filter the report by date range, app name, country, ad company, and ad format. After selecting filters, click **Submit** to load the report.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/Dashboard/ad-report.png"
        alt="Twinalyze Ad Report page"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review ad performance table">
    The report table shows daily ad performance with country, company, format, requested ads, loads, shows, failures, clicks, and performance percentages.

    Use this view to compare ad delivery quality and identify where ad performance is strong or weak.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize and export report">
    The right-side column panel helps you organize the table fields. You can also export the report as **PDF** or **CSV** from the export menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to read Ad Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select report filters">
    Choose the date range, app name, country, ad company, and ad format.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Submit">
    Apply the selected filters to generate the ad report.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review delivery metrics">
    Start with Requested, Load, Show, and Failed to understand ad delivery quality.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check engagement">
    Review Click and CTR to understand whether users are interacting with ads.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Compare by country and format">
    Compare rows by country, company, and format to find high-performing and low-performing ad areas.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export if needed">
    Download the report as PDF or CSV for sharing or further analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>
