User Engagement helps you understand how often users open your app and how frequently they return.

Use this page to measure repeat usage, recurring users, average sessions per user, and overall engagement quality.

## How to read this page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the date range">
    Choose the time period you want to analyze, such as Today, Yesterday, 7 Days, or a custom range.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose country">
    Select All countries or filter by a specific country to understand regional engagement.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review app open frequency">
    Check how many users opened the app once, multiple times, or became recurring users.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check average sessions">
    Use the line chart to understand how average sessions per user changed over time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Compare engagement quality">
    More recurring users and higher average sessions usually indicate stronger user engagement.
  </Step>
</Steps>
