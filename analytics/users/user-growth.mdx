User Growth helps you understand how your app is growing over time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review growth overview">
    The top section shows the main growth trend for the selected date range and country.

    You can compare **New Installs**, **Daily Uninstalls**, and **Total Uninstalls** in one chart. The summary numbers above the chart help you quickly understand total installed users, total uninstalled users, and users who uninstalled on the same day.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/User_userGrowth_1.PNG"
        alt="Twinalyze User Growth overview"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review app growth and uninstall rate">
    The lower section helps you understand the balance between installed and uninstalled users.

    The **App Growth** chart gives a quick install vs uninstall split, while **Uninstall Rate** shows uninstall activity by selected time format such as daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly.

    <Frame>
      <img

        alt="Twinalyze App Growth and Uninstall Rate"
      />

  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to use User Growth

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select date range">
    Choose the time period you want to analyze.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose country">
    Select All countries or filter by one country to understand market-specific growth.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review install and uninstall totals">
    Check Total Install User, Total Uninstall User, and Same Day Uninstall User.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Analyze the growth chart">
    Compare New Installs, Daily Uninstalls, and Total Uninstalls over time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check uninstall quality">
    Use Country Wise Uninstall, App Growth, and Uninstall Rate to understand where users are being lost.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common use cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Track app growth" icon="chart-line">
    Use New Installs and App Growth to understand whether your app is gaining users.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Find uninstall issues" icon="search">
    Use Daily Uninstalls, Total Uninstalls, and Uninstall Rate to find uninstall spikes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Review same-day uninstall" icon="clock">
    Same-day uninstall users can indicate poor onboarding, wrong user expectations, bugs, or low first-use value.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Compare countries" icon="globe">
    Use Country Wise Uninstall to find countries with higher uninstall percentage.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
