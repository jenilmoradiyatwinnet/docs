Page Insights helps you understand which screens or fragments users visit the most and how users move through your app or website.

Use this page to find high-traffic pages, compare screen performance, review active users, and understand average page views per user.

## How to use Page Insights

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select date range">
    Choose Today, Yesterday, 7 Days, or a custom date range based on the period you want to analyze.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose country">
    Select All countries or filter by a specific country to compare page performance by market.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Switch between Screen and Fragment">
    Use the **Screen** tab for full page or activity-level performance. Use the **Fragment** tab for smaller app sections or component-level views.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review top pages">
    Check total visits, active users, average page views, and remaining users to understand which screens are performing well.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use average page view card">
    Review the average number of pages viewed per user to understand whether users are exploring more pages or leaving quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
