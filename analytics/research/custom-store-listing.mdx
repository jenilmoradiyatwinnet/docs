## How to use Custom Store Listing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter app link or package ID">
    Paste the app link or package ID in the input field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the app">
    Click Submit to fetch listing information for the app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose country tier">
    Use All, T1, T1-1, T2, T3, or T4 to filter countries by market priority.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search country">
    Use the country search box when you want to find a specific country quickly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review listing data">
    Check language, logo, and app title in the result table.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export if needed">
    Use Export when you want to download or share the listing data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
