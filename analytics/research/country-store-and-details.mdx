Use these pages to compare countries, understand market priority, check local app store availability, and prepare country-wise research for app growth and market expansion.

## How to use these pages

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with Country Store">
    Use Country Store to browse countries visually and compare currency rate, population, and store availability.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a tier">
    Use T1-1, T1, T2, T3, or T4 to focus on the country group you want to research.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sort or search">
    Sort by country name, currency rate, or population. Use search when you need one specific country.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Country Details">
    Use Country Details when you need structured information like timezone, language, currency, and population.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use data for planning">
    Use the country data for pricing, localization, campaign scheduling, expansion planning, and app market research.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Empty state

If the page shows `No data`, it may mean the selected filter, tier, region, or search query does not currently have matching country data.

<Info>
  Try changing the region, tier, currency selector, or search keyword before assuming data is unavailable.
</Info>
