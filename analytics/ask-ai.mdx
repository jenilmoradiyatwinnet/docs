# Ask Twinalyze AI

Ask Twinalyze AI helps you explore your analytics data using simple questions.

Instead of opening multiple reports manually, you can ask questions like **“Why did retention drop?”**, **“Which users are most active?”**, or **“Show DAU for last 7 days”** and get an easy-to-read answer.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start from the Ask Twinalyze screen">
    The Ask Twinalyze home screen gives you a clean place to ask questions about your project.

    You can type your own question or start from suggested prompts such as retention, DAU, or most active users.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/ask_ai_1.png"
        alt="Twinalyze AI welcome screen"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review AI answers and generated data">

    <Frame>

      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best way to ask

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with the problem">
    Ask what you want to understand, such as retention drop, active users, traffic change, or country impact.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a time period">
    Add a date range like today, yesterday, last 7 days, or last week when your question depends on time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask for the format">
    Ask for a table, chart, summary, or explanation depending on how you want to review the answer.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask a follow-up">
    Continue with follow-up questions to narrow the answer by country, user type, version, campaign, or event.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Good to know

<Tip>
  Ask one clear question at a time. You can always ask follow-up questions after the first answer.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  Add context like date range, country, app version, or user type when you need a more accurate answer.
</Tip>

<Info>
  Twinalyze AI can make mistakes. Always review important answers before using them for business decisions, client reports, or production changes.
</Info>
