## What will be available

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Send Twinalyze events to GA4">
    Forward selected Twinalyze events into Google Analytics 4 for centralized campaign and behavior analysis.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Map event names and properties">
    Match Twinalyze event names and properties with your GA4 event structure.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Analyze traffic and campaigns">
    Compare UTM, source, medium, and campaign performance between Twinalyze and GA4.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Improve reporting consistency">
    Keep marketing and product reporting aligned across tools.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Preparation checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Keep your GA4 property ready">
    Make sure your GA4 property is created and active.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Prepare event naming">
    Decide which Twinalyze events should be sent to GA4.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review event properties">
    Keep important properties like campaign, source, user type, screen name, and country consistent.
  </Step>
</Steps>
