Twinalyze reads reporting data from the AdMob account you select, including ad requests, impressions, clicks, revenue, and other monetization metrics.


## Connect AdMob

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Open your Twinalyze project and go to **Project Setting → Integrations**.

    Find **AdMob** under the **Marketing** section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the AdMob integration">
    Select **AdMob** from the integrations page.

    Review how Twinalyze uses your AdMob reporting data, then click **Connect with Google AdMob**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a Google account">
    Select the Google account that has access to the AdMob publisher account you want to connect.

    Select **Use another account** to sign in with a different Google account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Allow reporting access">
    Review the requested Google permissions and continue.

    Twinalyze requests reporting access so it can read AdMob account and monetization data.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the AdMob account">
    Return to Twinalyze and select the required AdMob publisher account.

    Select an application if the option is available, then choose the historical data period you want to import.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the integration">
    Review the selected account and historical data period.

    Click **Submit** to complete the AdMob integration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
