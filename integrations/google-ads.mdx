Twinalyze reads reporting metrics such as impressions, clicks, cost, and conversions from the Google Ads account you select.


## Connect Google Ads

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    In Twinalyze, open your project and go to **Project Setting → Integrations**.
    Find **Google Ads** under the **Marketing** section.

    <Frame>
      ![Twinalyze integrations page showing Google Ads](/images/integrations-googleads/integrations.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start the connection">
    Select **Google Ads** from the integrations page.

    Review the information about how Twinalyze uses your Google Ads data, then click **Connect with Google Ads**.

    <Frame>
      ![Connect Google Ads with Twinalyze](/images/integrations-googleads/integrations-2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a Google account">
    Select the Google account that has access to the Google Ads account you want to connect.

    <Frame>
      ![Choose a Google account to connect with Twinalyze](/images/integrations-googleads/integrations-3.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the Google Ads account">
    After authorization, return to Twinalyze and select the required **Customer ID**.Choose the historical data period you want to import.
    Click **Submit** to complete the integration.

    <Frame>
      ![Select Google Ads customer ID and historical data period](/images/integrations-googleads/integrations-4.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Data imported from Google Ads

Twinalyze can read reporting data for the selected Google Ads account, including:

- Impressions
- Clicks
- Advertising cost
- Conversions
- Campaign performance
- Historical reporting data for the selected period
