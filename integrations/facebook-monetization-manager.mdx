Twinalyze imports reporting data from the Meta business and monetized property you select.

## Connect Meta Monetization Manager

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Open your Twinalyze project and go to **Project Setting → Integrations**.

    Find **Meta Monetization Manager** under the **Marketing** section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the integration">
    Select **Meta Monetization Manager** from the integrations page.

    Review how Twinalyze uses your monetization reporting data, then click **Connect with Meta**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in with Meta">
    Sign in using the Meta or Facebook account that has access to the required Monetization Manager property.

    Use another account when the displayed account does not have the required access.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review permissions">
    Review the permissions requested by Twinalyze and continue.

    These permissions allow Twinalyze to retrieve available businesses, properties, placements, and reporting data.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a monetization property">
    Return to Twinalyze and select the required Meta business and monetization property.

    Select the correct property when your account manages multiple applications.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select historical data">
    Choose the historical reporting period you want to import.

    The available historical period depends on the options provided in your Twinalyze project.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the integration">
    Review the selected business, property, and historical data period.

    Click **Submit** to complete the integration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
