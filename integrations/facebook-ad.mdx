Twinalyze imports reporting data from the selected Meta ad account, including impressions, clicks, reach, spend, conversions, and campaign performance.


## Connect Meta Ads

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Open your Twinalyze project and go to **Project Setting → Integrations**.
    Find **Meta Ads** under the **Marketing** section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the Meta Ads integration">
    Select **Meta Ads** from the integrations page.
    Review how Twinalyze uses your advertising data, then click **Connect with Meta Ads**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in with Meta">
    Sign in using the Facebook or Meta account that has access to the required ad account.
    Continue with another account when the currently displayed account does not have the required access.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review permissions">
    Review the requested permissions and continue.
    These permissions allow Twinalyze to retrieve advertising accounts and reporting data from Meta.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select an ad account">
    After authorization, return to Twinalyze and select the Meta ad account you want to connect.
    Make sure you select the correct ad account ID when your Meta account manages multiple accounts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select historical data">
    Choose the historical data period you want to import.
    The available options may include periods such as 7 days, 14 days, 30 days, or 90 days.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the integration">
    Review the selected ad account and historical data period.
    Click **Submit** to complete the Meta Ads integration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
