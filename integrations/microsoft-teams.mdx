## What will be available

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Send alerts to Teams">
    Push important product, analytics, and campaign alerts to Teams channels.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Share report summaries">
    Send useful reports to stakeholders without opening the dashboard.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Support internal workflows">

  <Card title="Improve response time">

## Preparation checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Teams channels">
    Decide which channels should receive Twinalyze updates.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Define alert categories">
    Prepare categories such as errors, campaigns, retention, ad failures, and traffic changes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review team access">
    Make sure the right team members can view the shared updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
