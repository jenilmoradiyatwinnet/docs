## Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Twinalyze collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our website, dashboard, SDKs, APIs, and related services.

<Check>
  Our goal is to collect only the data needed to provide analytics, improve product performance, support customers, and keep the platform secure.
</Check>

### Information we may collect

Twinalyze may collect different types of information depending on how you use the platform.

| Data type | Examples | Why we collect it |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Account information | Name, email, company name, role | To create and manage your account |
| Product usage data | Pages visited, features used, session activity | To improve the platform experience |
| Analytics data | Events, screens, sessions, device details | To provide analytics and reports |
| Technical data | IP address, browser, device, OS, logs | To secure and maintain the service |
| Billing data | Plan, billing status, payment reference | To manage subscriptions and invoices |
| Support data | Messages, tickets, feedback | To provide customer support |

We use collected information to operate and improve Twinalyze.

### How we share information

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information only when needed to operate the service.

| Shared with | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Service providers | Hosting, infrastructure, email, payments, support, and analytics operations |
| Legal authorities | When required by applicable law or valid legal process |
| Business transfers | If Twinalyze is involved in a merger, acquisition, or restructuring |
| Customer-authorized integrations | When you connect third-party tools or integrations |

<Tip>
  If you connect an integration, data may be exchanged based on your configuration and the permissions granted by you.
</Tip>

### Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

Security measures may include:

- Access controls
- Authentication and authorization
- Encrypted communication where applicable
- Logging and monitoring
- Infrastructure security practices
- Limited internal access based on role and need

### Data retention

We keep information only for as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required by law, billing rules, dispute resolution, or security needs.

| Data category | Typical retention purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Account data | Kept while your account is active |
| Analytics data | Kept based on your plan, settings, or agreement |
| Billing data | Kept for accounting, tax, and compliance needs |
| Support records | Kept to resolve issues and improve service |
| Security logs | Kept for security monitoring and investigation |

### Your choices and rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights related to your personal data.

These may include:

- Request access to your data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of certain data
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
- Request a copy of your data where applicable
