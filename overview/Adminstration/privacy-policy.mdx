## Cookie Policy

Twinalyze may use cookies and similar technologies to improve website experience, secure sessions, understand usage, and support product performance.

Cookies are small files stored on your browser or device. They help websites remember sessions, preferences, and activity.

### Cookie types we may use

| Cookie type | Purpose | Required |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Essential cookies | Login, security, sessions, and platform access | Yes |
| Preference cookies | Remember settings such as language or display choices | Optional |
| Analytics cookies | Understand visits, usage patterns, and page performance | Optional |
| Performance cookies | Measure loading speed, errors, and reliability | Optional |
| Marketing cookies | Measure campaigns and improve communication relevance | Optional |

### How cookies help Twinalyze

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep the platform working" icon="lock">
    Essential cookies help users sign in, stay authenticated, access dashboards, and use secure platform features.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Improve user experience" icon="sparkles">
    Preference cookies help remember selected settings so users get a smoother experience across visits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Understand website activity" icon="bar-chart">
    Analytics cookies help us understand which pages are visited, which content is useful, and where improvements are needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Measure performance" icon="gauge">
    Performance cookies help us find slow pages, errors, and reliability issues so we can improve the product.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  If you block essential cookies, login, dashboard access, and some secure features may not work correctly.
</Warning>

## Refund & Cancellation Policy

This policy explains how cancellations, refunds, subscription renewals, billing changes, and refund requests are handled.

<Info>
  Refund and cancellation eligibility may depend on your plan, billing cycle, usage, payment method, and any custom agreement with Twinalyze.
</Info>

### Cancellation summary

| Item | Explanation |
| --- | --- |
| Cancel subscription | You may cancel based on your plan settings or by contacting support. |
| Access after cancellation | Paid access may continue until the end of the current billing cycle. |
| Renewal stop | Future renewals usually stop after cancellation is confirmed. |
| Data access | Data access may depend on your plan, retention settings, and account status. |

### Refund eligibility

| Case | Refund possibility |
| --- | --- |
| Duplicate payment | Usually eligible after verification |
| Accidental payment | May be reviewed if reported quickly |
| Technical issue caused by Twinalyze | May be reviewed for partial or full refund |
| Unused subscription period | Depends on plan and billing terms |
| Custom enterprise plan | Depends on the signed agreement |
| Violation of terms | Usually not eligible |

### Usually non-refundable

- Already-used billing periods
- Heavy usage during the active subscription period
- Late cancellation after renewal
- Account suspension due to policy violation
- Custom setup, onboarding, or implementation fees
- Third-party payment gateway charges where applicable

### Request process

To request a cancellation or refund, prepare:

- Account email
- Organization or project name
- Invoice or payment reference
- Reason for cancellation or refund
- Screenshots or payment proof, if needed
