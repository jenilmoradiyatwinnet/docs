## Community guidelines

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be clear and specific" icon="message-circle">
    Explain your question with enough context. Include platform, SDK version, feature name, and steps if the issue is technical.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do not share sensitive data publicly" icon="shield">
    Avoid posting API keys, secrets, personal data, billing details, customer data, or private screenshots in public channels.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use the right channel" icon="route">
    Use Slack or Discord for general questions. Use email support when the request includes private account, billing, or security information.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Keep discussions respectful" icon="heart">
    Be respectful to other community members and support team members. Helpful and clear discussions make the community better for everyone.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
