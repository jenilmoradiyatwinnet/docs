Settings helps you manage your personal profile, organization details, security preferences, login activity, users, and permissions from one place.

Use this page when you want to update account information, invite team members, assign roles, or review access inside your Twinalyze workspace.

## How to use Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to Settings from the left sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update profile details">
    Use Manage Profile to update profile picture, personal details, and address details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review security">
    Open Security & Settings or Login History to check account security and access activity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite team members">
    Go to Users and Permissions, enter email, select role type, and click Invite.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review user access">
    Use status, role, and search filters to find users and review their access.
  </Step>
</Steps>
