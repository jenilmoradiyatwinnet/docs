Retention shows how many users come back after their first visit.

## Why it matters

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="User loyalty" icon="heart">
    Measure how engaged users are.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Product success" icon="chart-line">
    High retention means product value.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Example

Day 1 → 100 users  
Day 2 → 40 users return  

➡️ Retention = 40%

## How to improve

- Improve onboarding  
- Send notifications  
- Fix drop-offs
