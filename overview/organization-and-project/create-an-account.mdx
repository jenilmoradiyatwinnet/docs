## Edit organization

Go to **Settings → Manage Profile → Organization Profile**.

Update your organization name, email address, mobile number, website, or logo, and click **Save**.

---

## Delete organization

To delete your organization, send a request from your registered email address to [support@twinalyze.com](mailto:support@twinalyze.com).

<Warning>
  Organization deletion permanently removes related projects, users, settings, and analytics data. Back up important information before submitting your request.
</Warning>

## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a valid business email">
    Use an email address that your team can access. A business email is recommended when creating an account for a company.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Create a strong password">
    Use a secure password and do not share your account credentials with other team members.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use a clear organization name">
    Use your company, brand, or product name so team members can identify the organization easily.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invite each team member separately">
    Invite each team member and assign the appropriate role and permissions instead of sharing one account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Keep organization details updated">
    Update your organization name, contact information, website, and logo whenever your business information changes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Edit Organization

Use this section when you want to update your organization profile.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the **Setting** option from the left sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Manage Profile">
    Open **Manage Profile** and select the **Organization Profile** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update organization details">
    Edit organization name, email, mobile number, website, or upload organization logo.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save** to update the organization details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![Edit organization details in Twinalyze](/images/Organization_edit.PNG)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Keep your organization details updated so your team can easily identify and manage the correct organization.
</Tip>

## Delete Organization

Organization deletion is not directly available from the dashboard.

<Warning>
  To delete an organization, contact the Twinalyze support team. Organization deletion may remove related data permanently.
</Warning>

Use the support email shown in your organization settings screen, or contact:

```txt
support@twinalyze.com
```

<Info>
  Before requesting organization deletion, make sure the organization is no longer required and your team has backed up any important data.
</Info>
