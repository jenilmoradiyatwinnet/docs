<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Real-Time Analytics" icon="chart-line">
    Monitor live users, active sessions, screen views, and real-time dashboards.

    Best for live activity monitoring, app usage tracking, and campaign impact analysis.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="User & Event Tracking" icon="users">
    Track automatic events, user journeys, screen views, custom events, and engagement insights.

    Best for understanding user behavior and product usage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ads Analytics" icon="eye">
    Analyze impressions, clicks, revenue, failures, and ad performance.

    Best for improving ad revenue and reducing failed ad loads.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Push Notifications & Automation" icon="bell">
    Create scheduled notifications, multilingual campaigns, automated messages, and engagement tracking.

    Best for re-engagement, campaign automation, and user retention.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Funnels, Retention & Reports" icon="filter">
    Build conversion funnels, retention reports, cohort analysis, custom reports, and user segments.

    Best for finding drop-offs, measuring retention, and improving growth.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
