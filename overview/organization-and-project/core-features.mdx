## How Twinalyze works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the SDK">
    Add Twinalyze SDK to your app, website, or platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Collect product data">
    Twinalyze starts collecting users, sessions, screens, events, ads, and notification activity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Analyze behavior">
    Use dashboards, journeys, funnels, retention, cohorts, and reports to understand users.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Engage users">
    Send scheduled, multilingual, and automated campaigns.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Improve growth">
    Use insights to improve engagement, conversion, retention, and revenue.
  </Step>
</Steps>
