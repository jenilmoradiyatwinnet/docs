  <Tab title="Real-Time Analytics">

    ## Real-Time Analytics

    See what's happening in your app **right now**. Real-time analytics gives you a live window into user activity — no page refresh, no delay. Whether you just pushed an update or launched a campaign, you'll know the impact within seconds.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Live Users" icon="users">
        See exactly how many users are actively using your app at this moment. Useful for monitoring spikes after a release or marketing push.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Sessions" icon="signal">
        Track how many sessions are currently open — including session duration and where each session started.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Screen Views" icon="eye">
        Know which screens users are on right now. Identify your most visited screens in real time and spot unexpected drop-offs instantly.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Realtime Dashboard" icon="chart-line">
        All live metrics in one place — users, sessions, and screen activity consolidated into a single auto-updating dashboard.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Note>
      Real-time data typically appears on the dashboard within **5 seconds** of the event occurring in your app.
    </Note>

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="User & Event Tracking">

    ## User & Event Tracking

    Understand exactly what your users do inside your app. From the first screen they open to every button they tap, every interaction is captured and organized into clear, actionable insights — automatically.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Automatic Event Tracking" icon="bolt">
        Common events like button taps, screen opens, and app launches are tracked out of the box — no extra code needed.
      </Card>
      <Card title="User Journeys" icon="route">
        Visualize the exact path each user takes through your app — from entry to exit — and discover the most common flows.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Screen Tracking" icon="mobile">
        Every screen visit is logged with timestamps, giving you a full history of what each user has seen and when.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Custom Events" icon="code">
        Define and track events specific to your app — like a subscription upgrade, a form submission, or a feature unlock.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Engagement Insights" icon="fire">
        Measure how deeply users engage — session frequency, time spent per screen, and interaction rates across your app.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Tip>
      Use **Custom Events** alongside automatic tracking to get the most complete picture of user behavior without writing repetitive instrumentation code.
    </Tip>

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Ads Analytics">

    ## Ads Analytics

    Get complete visibility into how your in-app ads are performing. Track every impression, click, and rupee of revenue — and use that data to optimize placements and eliminate failures before they cost you.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Impressions" icon="rectangle-ad">
        Count how many times each ad unit was displayed to users, broken down by placement, format, and time period.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Clicks" icon="cursor">
        Track click-through rates per ad unit to understand which creatives and placements drive the most user interaction.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Revenue" icon="coins">
        Monitor ad revenue in real time — by network, placement, or date — so you always know where your earnings are coming from.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Failures" icon="triangle-exclamation">
        Detect when ads fail to load, time out, or return errors. Fix broken placements before they silently drain your fill rate and revenue.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Ad Performance Optimization" icon="chart-bar">
        Compare performance across networks and placements side by side, then make data-driven decisions on where to focus your inventory.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Warning>
      High **failure rates** on a placement usually indicate a misconfigured ad unit ID or a network issue on the device. Check the Failures report first before contacting your ad network.
    </Warning>

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Push Notifications">

    ## Push Notifications & Automation

    Reach your users at the right moment with the right message. Schedule campaigns in advance, send in their language, and let automation handle delivery — while built-in engagement tracking tells you what's actually working.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Scheduled Notifications" icon="clock">
        Plan and schedule push notifications days or weeks in advance. Set exact delivery times and let the system handle the rest.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Multilingual Campaigns" icon="language">
        Send the same campaign in multiple languages simultaneously. Each user receives the notification in their preferred language automatically.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Automated Messaging" icon="robot">
        Trigger notifications based on user behavior — like inactivity, a completed purchase, or a dropped session — without any manual effort.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Engagement Tracking" icon="chart-pie">
        See delivery rates, open rates, and click-through rates for every notification. Know which messages drive users back to your app.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Tip>
      Combine **Automated Messaging** with **User Journeys** to trigger notifications at exactly the right point in a user's lifecycle — for example, re-engaging users who dropped off mid-onboarding.
    </Tip>

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Funnels & Retention">

    ## Funnels, Retention & Reports

    Go beyond raw numbers. Understand *where* users drop off, *how long* they keep coming back, and *which groups* behave differently — then build custom reports to share exactly the metrics your team cares about.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Conversion Funnels" icon="filter">
        Define a sequence of steps — like Sign Up → Onboarding → First Purchase — and see what percentage of users complete each step. Pinpoint exactly where you're losing them.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Retention Analytics" icon="repeat">
        Measure how many users return after Day 1, Day 7, and Day 30. Retention is the single most important signal of long-term app health.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Cohort Analysis" icon="layer-group">
        Group users by install date, device, or any custom attribute — then compare how each cohort behaves over time.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Custom Reports & Segmentation" icon="sliders">
        Build reports around any combination of events, user properties, and time ranges. Segment by country, device, version, or your own custom attributes.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Note>
      **Cohort Analysis** is especially powerful when combined with push notification data — you can see whether a specific campaign actually improved retention for a given user group.
    </Note>

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="SDK Integration">

    ## Easy SDK Integration

    Getting started takes minutes, not days. The SDK is lightweight, non-intrusive, and starts collecting data automatically the moment it's initialized — no complex configuration required.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Lightweight SDK" icon="feather">
        Minimal overhead on your app's size and memory. Runs silently in the background without affecting user experience.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Fast Setup" icon="rocket">
        Initialize with a single line of code using your project API key. All core tracking begins automatically from that point.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Android & iOS Support" icon="mobile-screen">
        Full native support for both platforms — one consistent API across Android (Kotlin/Java) and iOS (Swift/Objective-C).
      </Card>
      <Card title="Auto Data Collection" icon="database">
        Sessions, screen views, device info, and lifecycle events are collected automatically — no manual calls needed for standard data.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Install the SDK">
        Add the SDK dependency via Gradle (Android) or CocoaPods / Swift Package Manager (iOS).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Initialize with your API key">
        Call `initialize()` in your `Application` class or `AppDelegate` with your project API key.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify data flow">
        Open the Realtime Dashboard and confirm events are appearing. You should see your first session within seconds.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add custom events (optional)">
        Use the `track()` method anywhere in your codebase to send events specific to your app's business logic.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Check>
      Once initialized, **all 6 feature modules** — analytics, event tracking, ads, push notifications, funnels, and reports — are active by default. No additional setup per feature is required.
    </Check>
