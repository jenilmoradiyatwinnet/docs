You can track users, analyze behavior, monitor realtime activity, and measure performance.

## Analytics screens

<Steps>
  <Step title="Realtime Users">
    This page shows **live activity** in your product.

    You can monitor:
    - Active users (last 30 minutes)
    - Live map (country-wise users)
    - App open frequency
    - App version usage

    <div className="not-prose my-4 rounded-xl border border-gray-200 bg-gray-50 p-3">
      <img src="/images/realtime_user.PNG" alt="Realtime users" className="rounded-lg" />
    </div>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Product Overview">
    This is your **main analytics dashboard**.

    You can analyze:
    - New users
    - Active users
    - Retention users
    - Average session duration
    - User acquisition (UTM / source)

    <div className="not-prose my-4 rounded-xl border border-gray-200 bg-gray-50 p-3">
      <img src="/images/product_overview_page.PNG" alt="Product overview" className="rounded-lg" />
    </div>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Users">
    This page shows **detailed user data**.

    You can:
    - Filter by date and country
    - Search users
    - Check app version and brand
    - View last activity and registration date

    <div className="not-prose my-4 rounded-xl border border-gray-200 bg-gray-50 p-3">
      <img src="/images/user_page.PNG" alt="Users page" className="rounded-lg" />
    </div>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ad Reports">
    This page helps you track **ad performance**.

    You can measure:
    - Requested, loaded, shown ads
    - Paid and failed ads
    - Clicks and CTR
    - Show rate

    <div className="not-prose my-4 rounded-xl border border-gray-200 bg-gray-50 p-3">
      <img src="/images/Ad_report.PNG" alt="Ad report" className="rounded-lg" />
    </div>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to use analytics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select filters">
    Choose date range (Today, 7 Days, custom) and country.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check overview">
    Start with Product Overview to understand overall performance.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Analyze realtime">
    Use Realtime Users to verify live activity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore users">
    Go to Users page for detailed insights.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Measure performance">
    Use reports to understand growth, retention, and ads.
  </Step>
</Steps>
