## How each tool helps

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Notifications">
    Notifications help you send updates, offers, reminders, and re-engagement messages.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Personalized messages" icon="message">
        Create notification content with title, description, logo, and destination.
      </Card>

      <Card title="Preview before sending" icon="mobile-screen">
        See how the notification will look on a device before saving.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Campaigns">
    Campaigns help you measure whether users are responding to your messages.

    | Metric | Meaning |
    | --- | --- |
    | **Sent** | Notifications sent to users |
    | **Delivered** | Notifications received by devices |
    | **Opened** | Users who opened notification |
    | **Clicked** | Users who clicked the notification |
    | **Conversion** | Users who completed the target action |
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Ads">
    Ad analytics helps you understand ad health and monetization performance.

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Requested">
        Number of times an ad was requested from the ad provider.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Loaded">
        Number of ads successfully loaded.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Failed">
        Number of ad requests that failed due to provider, network, or configuration issues.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="CTR and Show Rate">
        Helps you understand user interaction and ad visibility performance.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Research">
    Research helps you understand market demand and category trends.

    <Check>Compare countries and categories.</Check>
    <Check>Track top free, top paid, and grossing apps.</Check>
    <Check>Find opportunities for product and marketing strategy.</Check>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Common workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose audience or filter">
    Select users by event, date range, country, or behavior.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create engagement action">
    Send a notification, analyze ad performance, or explore research data.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Measure response">
    Review opens, clicks, conversions, revenue, failures, or trend changes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Improve next action">
    Use insights to improve message content, timing, targeting, and campaign strategy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

<Check>Use clear notification titles and short descriptions.</Check>
<Check>Always preview notifications before saving.</Check>
<Check>Compare campaign results by date and country.</Check>
<Check>Use ad failure insights to fix monetization issues.</Check>
<Check>Use research data before planning new markets or categories.</Check>

<Warning>
If engagement reports show no data, check SDK setup, notification permission, campaign filters, date range, and selected country.
</Warning>
