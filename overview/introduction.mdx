Twinalyze is an analytics and engagement platform for apps, websites, ads, and notifications. Twinalyze helps businesses understand **what users do**, **where they drop off**, and **which actions drive growth**.

It connects data from SDKs, ads, notifications, and third-party tools into one clear analytics platform.
