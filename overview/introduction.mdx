Twinalyze is an analytics and engagement platform for apps, websites, ads, and notifications.

## What is Twinalyze?

Twinalyze helps businesses understand **what users do**, **where they drop off**, and **which actions drive growth**.

It connects data from SDKs, ads, notifications, and third-party tools into one clear analytics platform.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Collect Data" icon="database">
    Bring user, event, session, campaign, and notification data into one place.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Find Insights" icon="chart-line">
    Identify user behavior, product issues, campaign performance, and drop-off points.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Improve ROI" icon="target">
    Use analytics to improve conversion, retention, engagement, and business results.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  Twinalyze is built for teams that want simple, clear, and actionable analytics across apps, websites, ads, and engagement channels.
</Info>

## How Twinalyze Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add SDK or integration">
    Add Twinalyze SDK to your app, website, backend, or supported platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track users and events">
    Twinalyze collects events, sessions, user properties, device details, and source data.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Analyze behavior">
    Use dashboards and reports to understand product usage, ads performance, and notification activity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Improve decisions">
    Use insights to improve user experience, reduce drop-offs, increase engagement, and improve ROI.
  </Step>
</Steps>
