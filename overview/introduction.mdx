Twinalyze is an analytics and engagement platform built to help businesses understand how users interact with their apps and websites. It helps teams track user journeys, sessions, events, ads performance, notifications, and user behavior from one place.

With Twinalyze, product, marketing, and development teams can understand where users come from, what actions they perform, where they drop off, and how to improve conversions and ROI.

## What Twinalyze helps you do

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Understand user behavior" icon="chart-line">

  </Card>

## Why Twinalyze?

Twinalyze gives businesses a clear view of what users are doing inside their app or website. Instead of only showing numbers, Twinalyze helps teams understand user problems, user journeys, campaign performance, and engagement gaps.

<Card title="Goal of Twinalyze" type="tip" icon="sparkles">
  The main goal of Twinalyze is to help businesses make better decisions, improve user experience, and increase ROI using clear analytics and engagement insights.
