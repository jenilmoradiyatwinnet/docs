Twinalyze is an analytics and engagement platform built to help businesses understand how users interact with their apps, websites, ads, and notifications.

It helps teams track user behavior, events, sessions, traffic sources, ad performance, notification engagement, and product usage from one place. With Twinalyze, businesses can understand where users come from, what they do, where they drop off, and how to improve conversion and ROI.

## What is Twinalyze?

Twinalyze gives product, marketing, and development teams a clear view of how users behave inside digital products.

Instead of only showing numbers, Twinalyze helps teams understand:

- Which users are active
- Which events are being performed
- Which screens or pages are important
- Which campaigns bring valuable users
- Where users leave before conversion
- How ads and notifications perform
- How to improve product experience and ROI

<Info>
  Twinalyze is designed for businesses that want simple, clear, and actionable analytics across apps, websites, ads, and engagement channels.
</Info>

## How Twinalyze Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add SDK or integration">
    Add Twinalyze SDK to your app, website, or supported platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track users and events">

  <Step title="Analyze behavior">
