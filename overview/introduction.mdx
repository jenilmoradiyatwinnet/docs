Twinalyze is an analytics and engagement platform that helps businesses understand how users interact with their apps, websites, ads, and notifications.

It gives product, marketing, and development teams one place to track user behavior, events, sessions, traffic sources, ads performance, notification engagement, and product usage.

## What is Twinalyze?

Twinalyze helps you understand what users are doing inside your digital product.

Instead of only showing numbers, Twinalyze helps teams find answers to important questions:

- Where are users coming from?
- Which pages or screens are users visiting?
- Which events are users performing?
- Where are users dropping off?
- Which campaigns are bringing valuable users?
- How are ads and notifications performing?
- How can we improve conversion, retention, and ROI?

<Info>
  Twinalyze is built for teams that want clear, simple, and actionable analytics across apps, websites, ads, and engagement channels.
</Info>

## How Twinalyze Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add SDK or integration">
    Add Twinalyze SDK to your app, website, backend, or supported platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track users and events">
    Twinalyze collects events, sessions, user properties, device details, and source data.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Analyze behavior">
    Use dashboards and reports to understand product usage, ads performance, and notification activity.
  </Step>

  </Step>
