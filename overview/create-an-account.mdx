## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a valid business email">
    Use an email address that your team can access easily. A business email is recommended when setting up Twinalyze for a company or product.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Keep your login details secure">
    Use a strong password and avoid sharing your login credentials with other team members. Invite users separately when team access is needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Create an organization with a clear name">
    Use your company, brand, or product name as the organization name so your team can identify it easily.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use one organization for one company or business">
    Keep all related projects under the same organization. This makes user management, permissions, and settings easier.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
