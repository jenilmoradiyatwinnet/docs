# Create Your Twinalyze Account

Get started with Twinalyze in just a few steps.  
This guide helps you create your account, log in, create your organization, and set up your first project for **Web** or **Android**.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Quick setup" icon="rocket">
    Create your account and get started in a few minutes.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Web & Android" icon="monitor-smartphone">
    Follow separate setup instructions for both Web and Android platforms.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Project ready" icon="folder-plus">
    Create your first organization and project to start tracking data.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Before you begin

Make sure you have:

- A valid email address
- Access to the Twinalyze dashboard
- Basic information for your project
- Your platform choice: **Web** or **Android**

---

## Setup Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up using your email and password.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in to Twinalyze">
    Access your dashboard after successful account creation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create organization">
    Set up your organization to manage your projects.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first project">
    Add a project for Web or Android and configure it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start integration">
    Use your project credentials and begin SDK setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a clear organization name">
    Choose an organization name that matches your company or product group so your team can easily manage projects later.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Create separate projects for each platform">
    Keep Web and Android as separate projects for better data clarity and management.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Check project settings before SDK integration">
    Make sure your platform selection and project details are correct before starting SDK installation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invite your team later">
    Once your setup is complete, you can invite teammates and manage roles from the dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
