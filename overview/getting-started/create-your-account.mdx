---
title: "Create Your Account"
description: "Learn how to create or sign in to your Twinalyze account from web or mobile."
---

Create a Twinalyze account to access your workspace, select an organization, create projects, and start tracking product data.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Website">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Twinalyze website">
        Go to the Twinalyze website and click <strong>Get Started</strong> or <strong>Login</strong>.

        <div className="not-prose my-4 rounded-xl border border-gray-200 bg-gray-50 p-3">
          <img
            src="/images/Login.PNG"
            alt="Twinalyze website login entry"
            className="rounded-lg"
          />
          <p className="mt-3 text-center text-sm text-gray-500">
            Twinalyze website homepage
          </p>
        </div>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Enter your login details">
        Enter your email and password, then click <strong>Sign in</strong>. You can also continue with Google if it is enabled for your account.

        <div className="not-prose my-4 rounded-xl border border-gray-200 bg-gray-50 p-3">
          <img
            src="/images/login_email-pass_page.PNG"
            alt="Twinalyze login page"
            className="rounded-lg"
          />
          <p className="mt-3 text-center text-sm text-gray-500">
            Login with email and password
          </p>
        </div>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Create your first project">
        After login, create a project by entering a project name and clicking <strong>Save</strong>.

        <div className="not-prose my-4 rounded-xl border border-gray-200 bg-gray-50 p-3">
          <img
            src="/images/logged_in.PNG"
            alt="Create project after login"
            className="rounded-lg"
          />
          <p className="mt-3 text-center text-sm text-gray-500">
            Create project screen after login
          </p>
        </div>
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Mobile App">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Twinalyze app">
        Open the Twinalyze mobile app and choose <strong>Login</strong> or <strong>Get Started</strong>.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Sign in to your account">
        Enter your email and password, or continue with Google if available.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select organization">
        After login, select your organization or create a new one.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Create project">
        Create a project for your app or website. This project will contain your users, events, sessions, and reports.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
